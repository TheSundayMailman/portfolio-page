Acceptable Losses

An expense tracking app complete with graphs, email reminders, and billing history. Enter an income to compare against your monthly expenses. See all upcoming bills and mark them as paid (so they reappear on their next due date), all done in this convenient app.

Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, JS, React, Redux, Moment, Nivo, Big Calendar, Cron, and NodeMailer.

Pokémon Learning Center

An app for learning the names of some popular Pokémon. Designed to help with memorizing via repetition, this app uses an algorithm that determines which Pokémon should be reviewed based on a user's history of hits and misses. The more times a Pokémon is correctly guessed, the further back in the pile it goes.

Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, JS, React, Redux, Node, Express, MongoBD, and Mongoose.

About Me

In college, I took a career test that said I would excel as a "Space Marriage Lawyer". But since no one is getting marreid in space yet, I decided to marry something else together: programming and user experience. This stemmed from my first experience with a video game. The fantastic blend of programming and UX was not something I take for granted since.

Today, I strive to replicate that experience for someone else. Solving puzzles behind the scenes, keeping the code light and concise, and delivering an app that users can consume without thinking, all while working with a crew of fellow do'ers is what keeps me going.
