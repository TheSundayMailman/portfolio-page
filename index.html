Acceptable Losses

An expense tracking app complete with graphs, email reminders, and billing history. Enter an income to compare against your monthly expenses. See all your upcoming bills and mark them paid (so they set to be due on next due date), all done in this convenient app.

Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, JS, React, Redux, Moment, Nivo, Big Calendar, Cron, and NodeMailer.

Pokémon Learning Center

An app for learning the names of some popular Pokémon. Designed to help a user learn via repetition, this app stacks a list of Pokémon as cue cards and asks a user to guess its name. The more times name is correctly guessed, the further back in the stack it goes.

Tech Stack: HTML, CSS, JS, React, Redux, Node, Express, MongoBD, and Mongoose.

About Me

In college, I took a career quiz that said I would excel as a "Space Marriage Lawyer". But since no one is getting marreid in space yet, I decided to marry something else together: programming and user experience. This stemmed from my first experience with a video game. The fantastic blend of programming and UX was not something I take for granted since.

Today, I strive to replicate that experience for someone else. Solving puzzles behind the scene, keeping the code light and concise, and delivering an app that user can use without thinking, all the while working with a crew of fellow do'ers is what keeps me going.
